Cod in tomato sauce

Poached cod in tomato sauce comes together in 35 minutes, and is a delicious way to incorporate more fish into your diet.

Flakey cod in a tomato and olive sauce is an easy recipe to make for dinner this week, and an absolute must-make if you’re looking for new ways to eat fish. The cod comes out tender and melts in your mouth, and is packed with so much flavor. Cod fillets are seasoned with a little salt, pepper and Italian seasoning, then cooked in a simple tomato sauce made from canned whole tomatoes, sauteed shallots and garlic, olives and a few spices. The fish comes out so tender and flavorful, and is delicious paired with roasted potatoes or pearl couscous and a simple side salad.

Ingredients

  • cod–4 cod fillets 4 oz each
  • canned whole, peeled tomatoes– this will create the base of the sauce
  • kalamata olives, for a little added flavor
  • shallots and garlic, for some aromatics
  • spices– salt, pepper, Italian seasoning, red pepper flakes
  • olive oil, basil oil or chili oil

Step by step instructions

  1. Season the cod fillets with the spices
  2. Heat a large skillet on medium heat then drizzle in some olive oil
  3. Cook the shallots and garlic for a couple of minutes, until fragrant
  4. Pour in the canned tomatoes and spices and simmer for 5-6 minutes
  5. Gently mash the tomatoes and adjust any seasoning
  6. Place the fish fillets into the sauce with the olives then cover and cook on low for about 10 minutes
  7. Garnish with a drizzle of olive oil, fresh basil and parsley
